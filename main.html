Movie Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows a team of skilled thieves who steal secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. The protagonist, Dom Cobb (played by Leonardo DiCaprio), is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform "inception"—implanting an idea in someone's mind while they are dreaming.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, the expert in extracting secrets from the subconscious. Other notable actors include:

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Ellen Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer

Favorite Quote

One of the most memorable quotes from the movie is:

"You mustn't be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling." – Eames
This quote is a perfect reflection of the daring and ambitious nature of the characters in Inception.
